Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry

Method

1- Chop the garlic and ginger finely then dice the onion.

2-Peel the sweet potato and cut it into 2x2cm cubes and Cut the tomatoes into 1x1cm cubes.

3-Drain and rinse the chickpeas under running cold water and Heat the oil in a heavy bottom deep pan.

4-Add the onions and sauté for few minutes until slightly golden then Add the garlic, ginger and spices and sauté until fragrant

5-Put the sweet potatoes in the pan add water, enough to cover all the potatoes then Season with salt and pepper.

6-Bring it to a boil and let it cook for 10-15 minutes

7-Add the chickpeas, tomatoes and let it cook uncovered for 5-10 minutes

8-Once the potatoes are cooked through and starting to break slightly, add the coconut milk and adjust the seasoning.

9-Bring it to a boil, add coriander and turn off the heat.

10-Serve immediately with warm brown rice.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 400g can chickpeas
  • 1 large white sweet onion
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves >
  • thumb size piece of ginger
  • 2tbs olive oil
  • 1/4tsp turmeric
  • sea salt, pepper
  • 1/2tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 coconut milk
  • coriander leaves
  • brown rice to serve
