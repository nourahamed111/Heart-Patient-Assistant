Mushroom Cream Pasta

Method

1-Bring a large pot of water to a boil then Cook Rigatoni according to the packaging instructions. While it’s cooking make the sauce.

2-Heat the oil slightly in a large skillet and Sauté sliced mushrooms until slightly browned then Add garlic and sauté for couple of minutes longer

3-Add cashew cream and half of the water and cook until it starts bubbling.

4-With the heat the cashew cream thickens so you’ll need to adjust it with the rest of the water, you might need to add a little more or a little less.

5-Once you adjust the thickness of the sauce, season with sea salt and pepper.

6-Turn off the heat. Add the sauce into cooked Rigatoni mixing well.

7-Add arugula and parsley. Serve immediately, enjoy!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pack 500g Rigatoni
  • 500g button mushroom (sliced)
  • generous handful arugula (optional)
  • 200g Cashew Cream
  • parsley
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • olive oil
  • 1cup water
  • sea salt, pepper
  • juice of 2 lemon
